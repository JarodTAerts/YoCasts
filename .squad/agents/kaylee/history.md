## Core Context

**UI Architecture (finalized):**

- **Deep research: Media module & background services (2026-04-13):** Completed exhaustive research on Garmin Connect IQ's Media module, background services, and power management. Full reference written to `docs/garmin-media-and-background-research.md`. Key discoveries:
  1. **AudioContentProviderApp is a SEPARATE app type** — must extend `AudioContentProviderApp` (not `AppBase`). Launched from music widget, not app list. Different lifecycle: `getContentDelegate()`, `getSyncConfigurationView()`, `getSyncDelegate()`. This is how Spotify/Deezer/Amazon Music work. Our current device app CANNOT do audio download/playback.
  2. **SyncDelegate is THE download mechanism** — `makeWebRequest()` with `HTTP_RESPONSE_CONTENT_TYPE_AUDIO` and `:mediaEncoding` streams audio directly to encrypted disk cache, bypassing the ~32-100KB normal response size limit. Downloads are sequential (chained manually), cannot be parallelized or resumed.
  3. **`Media.SyncDelegate` and `Media.startSync()` are DEPRECATED after System 9** — use `Communications.SyncDelegate` instead (same interface). Programmatic sync triggering replacement is unclear.
  4. **Background services (64KB) CANNOT access Media module** — useful for metadata sync/position push only, not audio downloads.
